I just presented my poster at QUASY at SyntaxFest! My research, conducted with [Dr. Zoey Liu](https://zoeyliu18.github.io/) and [MA Valeria Pagliai](https://aclanthology.org/people/valeria-pagliai/unverified/), used computational methods to analyze Spanish-English code-switching in three bilingual corpora. Feel free to check out our [proceedings paper](https://aclanthology.org/2025.quasy-1.11/) to learn more.
