[New publication](https://revistes.uab.cat/isogloss/article/view/v11-n4-treadway-higdon-valdeskroff)! The paper that I have co-written with [MA Hannah Treadway](https://hannahtreadway.github.io/) and [Dr. Jorge Valdés Kroff](https://people.clas.ufl.edu/jvaldeskroff/) is officially published. We examined how L2 Spanish learners process light verb code switches, such as _hice drink the coffee._, that are uncommon in Florida to investigate how passive exposure to Spanish-English code-switching may affect their processing of light verb code switches. Special thanks to Hannah for welcoming me onto this project!
